About Me

Hello, I'm Fahad

I am a BS-MS Dual Degree student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Kolkata, majoring in Physics. My research interests span computational neuroscience, artificial intelligence, and biophysics.

Currently, I'm working with the MIT Computational Biology Group as part of the MIT AI Mantis Rising Scholar Program under Professor Manolis Kellis. My work focuses on creating and improving the MANTIS AI platform's landscapes for drug discovery, molecular dynamics, and patient trajectory analysis.

I'm passionate about applying computational methods to solve complex problems in science and healthcare. My background combines rigorous physics education with practical experience in programming, data analysis, and machine learning.
